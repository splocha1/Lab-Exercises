Welcome to Sofia's reads of 2023!

This site is to document the books that I've read in 2023 and what I think about them.

The first book that I read in 2023 was The Priory of the Orange Tree.

This book is nearly perfect. It's one of those books where you wish you could erase your memory just to read it again. I give the book 4.8 stars out of 5. The prequel, titled "A Day of Fallen Night", comes out February 28th, 2023. More reviews about Priory can be found here.

The second book that I read in 2023 was The Secret History.

This book is a rollercoaster. It's one of those books where you don't really understand it but thats what makes it cool. The characters were amazing and unique and the story will stick with you. I give the book a 4.4 out of 5. More reviews about The Secret History can be found here.
